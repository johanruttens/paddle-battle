Paddle Battle is a single-player arcade game that does not require login or account creation.

To test the game:
1. Launch the app - you will see the main menu
2. Tap "PLAY" to start Level 1
3. Swipe left/right to control the bottom paddle
4. Score points by getting the ball past the AI paddle at the top
5. First to reach the target score wins the level

Key features to review:
- Level progression (100 levels across 5 tiers)
- Difficulty selection (Easy/Medium/Hard on main menu)
- Statistics tracking (tap "STATISTICS" on main menu)
- Settings (tap "SETTINGS" to adjust sound/haptics)
- Level select (tap "LEVEL SELECT" to replay unlocked levels)

No in-app purchases or subscriptions.
No user accounts or login required.
No network connectivity required - fully offline game.
