# Privacy Policy for Paddle Battle

**Last Updated: December 23, 2024**

## Overview

Paddle Battle ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally

Paddle Battle stores the following data locally on your device only:

- **Game Progress**: Current level, unlocked levels, star ratings
- **Game Statistics**: Games played, wins, losses, total points scored
- **User Preferences**: Sound volume, haptic feedback settings, difficulty level

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is **never transmitted** to any external servers.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

Paddle Battle does **NOT** collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers
- Any data transmitted over the internet

## Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data:

- Remains on your device only
- Is not backed up to any cloud service by the app
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Can be reset within the app's settings

## Third-Party Services

Paddle Battle does **NOT** use any third-party services including:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Crash reporting services

## Children's Privacy

Paddle Battle is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. Your game progress stays on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

- Email: privacy@paddlebattle.app
- Website: https://paddlebattle.app/support

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can clear all local game data by:

1. Uninstalling the app, or
2. Using your device's app data clearing functionality

---

**Summary**: Paddle Battle is a fully offline game that stores your progress locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or share any personal information.
